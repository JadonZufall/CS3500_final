Previous Tours

Fearless Tour (2009-2010)

Tour Information:

In the Fearless Tour, her first tour as a headlining act, Taylor Swift visited six contries including the U.S., Canada, England, Austrialia, The Bahamas, and Japan, performing a total of 118 shows. The opening acts for the majority of the tour were Gloriana and Kellie Pickler, but for two shows in England and one in Foxborough, Justin Bieber opened. The total estimated attendance for the tour was about 1.2 million, grossing approximately $66,500,000.

Speak Now World Tour (2011-2012)

Tour Information:

Tylor Swift's second headlining tour was for her third album, 'Speak Now'. This tour included shows in many parts of the world including North America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania. The tour consisted of a total of 110 performances featuring several different opening acts in Asia and Europe, while the North American shows were accompanied by Needtobreathe, and the Oceania shows were accompanied by Hot Chelle Rae. Some shows of this tour also featured special guests such as Justin Bieber, Hayley Williams, Nicki Minaj, etc. Overall the tour had an estimated attendance od 1.64 million, and grossed approximately $123,700,000.

The Red Tour (2013-2014)

Tour Information:

Tylor Swift's third headlining tour was for her fourth studio album, 'Red'. The Red Tour largly retreaded the same ground as her previous tour in visiting North America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania. The tour included 86 perfomances, most of which with Ed Sheeran opening. Notable speacial guest appearances include Ellie Goulding, Luke Bryant, Rascal Flatts, Sam Smith, etc. The Red Tour also was nominated for several awards including 'Top Package' from Billboard Live Music Awards which it won. The tour had an estimated attendance od 1.7 million, and grossed approximately $150,200,000.

The 1989 World Tour (2015)

Tour Information:

Tylor Swift's fourth headlining tour was for her fifth studio album, '1989'. The 1989 World Tour once again returned to the same four regions as her previous tours, visiting North America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania. Over the 85 performances, Swift was joined by Vance Joy and Shawn Mendes for much of them. Other special guest include The Weeknd, Nick Jonas, Lorde, Jason Derulo, etc. Like The Red Tour, The 1989 World Tour also recieived award nominations, and won Best US Act from the MTV Europe Music Award, and Tour of the Year, and Best Tour from the iHeartRadio Music Awards. The tour had an estimated attendance od 2.28 million, and grossed approximately $250,700,000.

Reputation Stadium Tour (2018)

Tour Information:

Tylor Swift's fifth headlining tour was for her sixth studio album, 'Reputation'. Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour, dispite making stops in the same regions as the ones before, had notable fewer performances with only 53. For many of the 52 shows, she was joined by Camila Cabello and Charli XCX. There were also fewer special guest this tour when compared to previous with notable ones being Selena Gomez, Tim McGraw, Bryan Adams, etc. Continuing the trend of previous two tours, Reputation Stadium Tour, was nominated for eleven awards, winning seven of them. The tour had an estimated attendance od 2.939 million, and grossed approximately $345,700,000.
