Acting Career

In addition to being a musical super star, Taylor Swift has dipped her toes into the film world. Below are the films in which she made an appearance, as well as her role:

Valentine's Day (2010)

In the 2010 romantic comedy, Valentine's Day, Taylor Swift plays the role of Felicia Miller, a highschool athlete freshly in love with her teammate, Willy Harrington, played by Taylor Lautner.

The Lorax (2012)

In The Lorax, a 2012 animated film based on the childrens book of the same name, Taylor Swift plays Audrey, the primary love interest of the protagonist, Ted Wiggins, played by Zac Efron.

The Giver (2014)

In the 2014 dystopian drama, The Giver, Taylor Swift plays the character Rosemary, who after becoming Receiver of Memory is so distraught at what she finds she asks to be "released".

Cats (2019)

2019's film adaptation of popular musical Cat's included Taylor as the femme fatale charcter, Bombalurina. She assists the main antagonist by performing a song to distract everyone while spreading catnip to incapacitate them.

Amsterdam (2022)

Amsterdam, released in 2022, is the most recent film in which Taylor Swift appreared. In this myster comedy triller, she plays Elizabeth Meekins, a woman whose murder at the hands of a hitman is framed on the protagonists.
